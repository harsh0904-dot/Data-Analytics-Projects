# Executive KPI Template

## Best For

- C-level business summary pages
- Portfolio hero dashboards
- Monthly leadership review packs

## Recommended Visuals

- KPI cards
- Monthly trend chart
- Category contribution bar chart
- Region or department breakdown
- Slicer panel

## Core KPIs

- Revenue or total value
- Growth percentage
- Active customers or users
- Profit or margin
- Top-performing category
