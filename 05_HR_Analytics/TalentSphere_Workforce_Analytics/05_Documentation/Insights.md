# Insights

## Workforce Snapshot

- Total Employees: 1.47K
- Active Employees: 1.233K
- Employees Left: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%

## Attrition Patterns

- Research & Development has the largest attrition contribution by volume.
- Sales is the second-largest attrition contributor.
- Human Resources contributes the least absolute attrition count.
- Travel_Rarely and Travel_Frequently together account for most exits.

## Compensation and Level Trends

- Monthly income increases consistently with job level (L1 to L5).
- Higher-level roles show significantly higher average compensation.

## Department Comparison

- Sales shows the highest average monthly income among departments.
- Research & Development has strong performance rating with moderate work-life balance.
- Human Resources has the lowest average job satisfaction among the three departments.

## Action-Oriented Recommendations

- Prioritize retention interventions in R&D and Sales first.
- Analyze role-specific causes for Sales Representative and Sales Executive exits.
- Review travel policy and workload for frequent travelers.
- Launch focused engagement programs for low-satisfaction segments.
