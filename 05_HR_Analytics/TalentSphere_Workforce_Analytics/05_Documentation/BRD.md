# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

- Project: TalentSphere Workforce Analytics
- Domain: Employee Attrition and Workforce Performance
- Audience: HR Director, CHRO, Department Heads

## Problem Statement

- Attrition is high enough to impact hiring cost, team stability, and productivity.
- HR teams need fast visibility into where attrition is happening and why.

## Business Objectives

- Track total workforce, exits, and attrition rate.
- Identify attrition hotspots by department, role, and travel behavior.
- Compare department-level performance, satisfaction, and work-life balance.
- Enable drill-down analysis for better retention actions.

## Scope

- In scope: Descriptive analysis, KPI dashboard, department/role segmentation.
- Out of scope: Predictive attrition modeling and real-time HRIS integration.

## Success Criteria

- Dashboard answers all core HR attrition questions in one view.
- Headline metrics match dataset totals (1.47K employees, 237 exits, 16.12% attrition).
- Stakeholders can filter by department, role, travel, and attrition status.
