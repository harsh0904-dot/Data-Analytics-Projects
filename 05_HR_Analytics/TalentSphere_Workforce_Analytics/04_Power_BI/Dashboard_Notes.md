# Dashboard Notes

## Theme and Visual Style

- Background: dark canvas for executive presentation.
- Primary colors: blue (stable), yellow (attrition), orange (highlight), green (positive).
- Keep consistent fonts and card sizing across pages.

## Slicer Strategy

- Global slicers: Department, JobRole, BusinessTravel, Attrition.
- Keep slicers synced where cross-page filtering is needed.

## KPI Intent

- Total Employees: current workforce size.
- Active Employees: retained workforce.
- Employees Left: attrition volume.
- Attrition Rate: retention risk level.

## Reading the Pages

- Executive page: quick snapshot and distribution overview.
- Attrition page: where exits happen and contribution by segment.
- Performance page: quality, compensation, and balance comparison by department.

## QA Checklist

- KPI values match expected totals (1.47K, 1.233K, 237, 16.12%).
- Department totals match chart labels.
- Tooltips show clear labels and percentages.
- No broken interactions between slicers and visuals.
