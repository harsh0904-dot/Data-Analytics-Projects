# Insights & Recommendations

## Summary

This section captures business-ready findings from SQL analysis, Python outputs, and Power BI dashboard exploration.

## Key Insight Areas

### 1) Platform Performance

- Identify platform(s) with strongest ROAS and profit contribution.
- Compare high-spend platforms against conversion outcomes.

### 2) Campaign Objective Effectiveness

- Highlight objectives with best conversion efficiency.
- Flag objectives with high spend but weaker returns.

### 3) Audience Segment Opportunities

- Pinpoint age/gender/interest groups with highest revenue quality.
- Validate retargeting effectiveness by conversion and ROAS.

### 4) Time-Based Optimization

- Identify high-performing days and hours.
- Recommend schedule adjustments for budget concentration.

### 5) Budget Allocation

- Compare budget tiers by profit, ROAS, and CPA.
- Propose budget reallocation from low-efficiency to high-efficiency segments.

## Recommended Actions

1. Increase spend on high-ROAS platform-objective combinations.
2. Reduce spend on campaigns with persistent low conversion rate.
3. Prioritize audience segments with high conversion quality.
4. Shift campaign timing toward top-performing hours and days.
5. Track KPI deltas weekly to validate optimization impact.

## Monitoring Plan

- Weekly: Spend, Revenue, Profit, ROAS, CTR, Conversion Rate, CPA.
- Monthly: Segment-level trend review and budget shift decisions.
- Quarterly: Strategic objective and channel mix review.
