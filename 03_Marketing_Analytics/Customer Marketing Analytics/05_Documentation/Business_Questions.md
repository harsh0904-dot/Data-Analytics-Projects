# Business Questions

## Executive Questions

1. Which platforms generate the highest revenue and profit?
2. Are we achieving positive ROAS overall and by channel?
3. Which campaign objectives deliver the best conversion efficiency?

## Budget & ROI Questions

1. Which budget tier performs best in terms of ROAS and CPA?
2. Which campaigns consume high spend with low return?
3. Where should budget be reduced, maintained, or increased?

## Audience Questions

1. Which audience age and gender segments convert best?
2. Does retargeting improve conversion rate and ROAS?
3. Which interest categories are most profitable?

## Time-Based Questions

1. Which days of the week perform best?
2. What are the highest-performing hours of the day?
3. Are there seasonal or quarter-level performance differences?

## Creative & Engagement Questions

1. Which creative format and placement drive better CTR?
2. How does quality score relate to conversion and ROAS?
3. Does bounce rate correlate with lower conversions?
