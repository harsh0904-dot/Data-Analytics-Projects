# Insights

## Executive Highlights

- Total Patients: 984
- Total Treatment Cost: 8,233,600
- Average Cost: 8,367.48
- Insurance Claimed: 351

## Patient and State Trends

- Kerala has the highest patient count at 120.
- Delhi and Punjab follow as the next highest patient-contributing states.
- Patient volume is spread broadly across conditions with similar counts.

## Clinical Findings

- Recovered outcomes account for 591 patients or 60.06%.
- Stable outcomes account for 393 patients or 39.94%.
- Cancer has the highest average length of stay at 42.65 days.
- Prostate Cancer and Heart Attack also show long average stay durations.

## Financial Findings

- Cancer is the largest contributor to total treatment cost.
- Heart Attack, Heart Disease, and Childbirth are also major cost contributors.
- Insurance claims cover 35.67% of total patient records.

## Recommended Actions

- Review high-cost treatment pathways for Cancer and Heart Attack.
- Monitor long-stay conditions for bed and discharge planning.
- Improve insurance conversion and claim support for eligible patients.
