# Business Requirements Document

- Project: MediCare Healthcare Analytics
- Audience: Hospital executives, finance teams, and operations managers

## Problem Statement

- Hospital leaders need a single view of patient volume, treatment costs, outcomes, and insurance patterns.
- Current reporting does not clearly connect clinical and financial performance.

## Objectives

- Monitor patient admissions and treatment outcomes.
- Track total treatment cost and average cost trends.
- Analyze condition-wise cost drivers.
- Measure insurance claim distribution and clinical satisfaction.

## Success Criteria

- Dashboard shows clear KPI snapshot for executives.
- Clinical and financial pages support drill-down analysis.
- Metrics align with cleaned dataset totals and Power BI visuals.
