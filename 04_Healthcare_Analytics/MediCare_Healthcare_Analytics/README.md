# MediCare Healthcare Analytics

- Domain: Healthcare Analytics
- Focus: Patient trends, treatment outcomes, insurance claims, and hospital cost analysis
- Tools: MySQL, Python, Power BI

## Project Goal

- Build an executive healthcare dashboard for hospital operations and financial monitoring.
- Track patient volume, total treatment cost, clinical outcomes, and insurance claim patterns.

## Key Dashboard Metrics

- Total Patients: 984
- Total Cost: 8.23M
- Average Cost: 8.37K
- Total Insurance Claimed: 351
- Average Length of Stay: 37.66
- Average Satisfaction: 3.60

## Dashboard Pages

- Hospital Executive Overview
- Clinical & Patient Insights
- Financial & Hospital Performance

## Main Insights

- Kerala has the highest patient count.
- Cancer contributes the highest treatment cost.
- Recovered outcomes account for 60.06% of records.
- Insurance claimed patients account for 35.67% of total patients.

## Project Structure

- `01_Dataset`: Raw, cleaned, and dictionary files.
- `02_SQL`: KPI and business analysis queries.
- `03_Python`: Cleaning, EDA, and feature engineering scripts.
- `04_Power_BI`: Dashboard notes and build steps.
- `05_Documentation`: BRD, model, DAX, insights, and validation notes.
- `06_Screenshots`: Final dashboard screens.
