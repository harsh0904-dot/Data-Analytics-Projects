# Data Dictionary

- Dataset name: Hospital Patient
- Grain: One row per patient admission record
- Total rows: 984

## Column Reference

- `patient_id`: Unique patient record identifier.
- `age`: Patient age in years.
- `gender`: Patient gender.
- `condition`: Primary medical condition.
- `medication`: Treatment or medication assigned.
- `admission_date`: Hospital admission date.
- `discharge_date`: Hospital discharge date.
- `patient_state`: State of residence of the patient.
- `year_of_admission`: Admission year used for trend analysis.
- `length_of_stay`: Number of hospital stay days.
- `readmission`: Indicates whether the patient was readmitted.
- `outcome`: Final treatment outcome.
- `satisfaction`: Patient satisfaction rating.
- `insurance_claimed`: Indicates whether insurance was claimed.
- `total_cost`: Total treatment cost for the patient record.
