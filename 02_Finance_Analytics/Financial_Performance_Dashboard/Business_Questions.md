# Business Questions

This document defines the key business questions for the Financial Performance Dashboard based on the financial loan dataset.

## Strategic Questions
1. What is the overall health of the loan portfolio in terms of repayment vs. charge-off behavior?
2. Which borrower segments (grade, purpose, home ownership, verification status) create the highest repayment value?
3. Which segments create the highest credit risk and negative payment gap?
4. How does portfolio quality differ between 36-month and 60-month loans?
5. Which states contribute the largest share of funded amounts and total repayments?

## Operational Questions
1. What is the current monthly trend of issued loans and total funded amount?
2. Which loan statuses are increasing over time, especially Charged Off?
3. Are high installment loans associated with better or worse repayment performance?
4. How does debt-to-income ratio impact repayment outcomes?
5. Are Source Verified and Verified applications performing better than Not Verified applications?

## Analytical Drill-Down Questions
1. For each grade and sub-grade, what is the average interest rate, funded amount, and payment gap?
2. What is the default rate by purpose category (debt consolidation, credit card, small business, etc.)?
3. Which combinations of term + grade + verification status create the riskiest cohort?
4. Which states have high funding volumes but weak repayment quality?
5. Who are the top and bottom segments by net payment gap contribution?

## Decision Outcomes Expected
1. Prioritize low-risk, high-repayment borrower cohorts in future lending strategy.
2. Tighten underwriting controls for high-risk purpose and grade combinations.
3. Improve verification policy where repayment quality is clearly better.
4. Use state-level and term-level insights to optimize portfolio mix.
