# Customer Churn Case Study

## Project Overview

- Domain: Customer Analytics
- Focus: Customer retention, churn drivers, and high-risk segment analysis
- Tools: SQL, Python, Power BI

## Business Problem

- A subscription-based company is experiencing customer loss and needs to understand why churn is happening.
- Leadership wants clear visibility into which customers are leaving, what patterns are linked to churn, and where retention action should begin.

## Business Objective

- Identify the strongest churn drivers across customer profile, service usage, plan type, and tenure.
- Segment customers into low-risk and high-risk groups for targeted retention strategy.
- Translate churn analysis into practical business recommendations.

## Key Business Questions

- Which customer groups show the highest churn rate?
- Do contract type, tenure, or payment behavior influence churn?
- Which products or service bundles are associated with lower retention?
- Are there customer segments that should be prioritized for immediate retention efforts?
- What actions can reduce churn without increasing cost inefficiently?

## Suggested Analytical Approach

- Clean and standardize the raw churn dataset.
- Perform exploratory analysis on demographics, contracts, services, tenure, and churn behavior.
- Use SQL and Python to compare churned vs retained customers.
- Build a Power BI dashboard for churn overview, segment deep dive, and action tracking.

## Expected Deliverables

- Cleaned customer churn dataset
- SQL queries for KPI validation and segment analysis
- Python scripts for cleaning, EDA, and feature engineering
- Power BI dashboard for churn monitoring
- Insight summary with retention recommendations

## Expected Insights

- High-risk customer segments based on contract type, monthly charges, and tenure
- Product or service combinations linked to stronger retention
- Revenue impact of churned customers
- Retention opportunity areas with the strongest business value

## Business Recommendations

- Prioritize early-tenure customers with high churn probability.
- Review contract and pricing models for weak-retention customer groups.
- Design targeted retention campaigns for high-value customers at risk.
- Track churn KPIs monthly to measure intervention success.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Business problem framing
- Customer segmentation and KPI analysis
- SQL-based churn investigation
- Python-based EDA and feature engineering
- Power BI storytelling and recommendation design
