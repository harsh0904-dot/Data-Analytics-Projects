# Retail Revenue Case Study

## Project Overview

- Domain: Retail Analytics
- Focus: Revenue performance, product contribution, customer value, and regional sales trends
- Tools: SQL, Python, Power BI

## Business Problem

- A retail business is seeing uneven revenue growth across categories, regions, and months.
- Leadership needs a clear view of what is driving revenue, where performance is slowing, and which commercial areas need intervention.

## Business Objective

- Identify the strongest revenue drivers across products, regions, and customer segments.
- Detect underperforming markets and weak-performing categories.
- Translate sales analysis into practical actions that improve growth and profitability.

## Key Business Questions

- Which products and categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which regions are underperforming relative to others?
- Which time periods show sales slowdown or growth acceleration?
- Which customer segments generate the most value?
- What commercial actions would improve performance in weak areas?

## Suggested Analytical Approach

- Clean and standardize the retail transactions dataset.
- Analyze revenue, order behavior, customer value, and regional performance.
- Use SQL for KPI validation and business segmentation.
- Build a Power BI dashboard for executive revenue tracking and drill-down analysis.

## Expected Deliverables

- Clean retail sales dataset
- SQL queries for KPI and regional performance analysis
- Python scripts for data preparation and exploratory analysis
- Power BI revenue performance dashboard
- Executive recommendation summary

## Expected Insights

- Top-performing products and categories by revenue contribution
- Regions with slow growth or low conversion performance
- Customer segments with the highest revenue concentration
- Seasonal or monthly performance patterns affecting sales outcomes

## Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-margin and high-performing product categories.
- Reassess underperforming regions through pricing, assortment, or campaign strategy.
- Build targeted actions for the most valuable customer segments.
- Track revenue KPIs regularly to identify slowdowns early.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Revenue and KPI analysis
- Regional and category segmentation
- SQL-based business performance investigation
- Python-based data preparation and trend analysis
- Power BI storytelling for executive reporting

## Why This Case Study Is Portfolio-Ready

- Retail analytics is one of the most recognizable and practical business domains.
- The case study combines revenue storytelling with actionable growth strategy.
- It is easy to present because the problem, analysis, and recommendations are tightly connected.
