# KPI Catalog

## KPI 1: Total Sales

- Definition: Total revenue generated from all completed customer orders.
- Formula: SUM(Sales)
- Business Importance: Tracks overall business performance.

## KPI 2: Total Profit

- Formula: SUM(Profit)
- Business Importance: Measures overall profitability.

## KPI 3: Total Orders

- Formula: DISTINCTCOUNT(Order ID)
- Business Importance: Measures total customer transactions.

## KPI 4: Total Customers

- Formula: DISTINCTCOUNT(Customer ID)
- Business Importance: Shows customer base size.

## KPI 5: Profit Margin %

- Formula: Profit / Sales ×100
- Business Importance: Measures business efficiency.

## KPI 6: Average Order Value

- Formula: Sales / Orders
- Business Importance: Measures customer spending.

## KPI 7: Average Discount %

- Formula: AVERAGE(Discount)
- Business Importance: Evaluates discount strategy.

## KPI 8: Average Profit per Order

- Formula: Profit / Orders
- Business Importance: Shows profitability of each transaction.

## KPI 9: Total Quantity Sold

- Formula: SUM(Quantity)
- Business Importance: Measures sales volume.

## KPI 10: Number of Products Sold

- Formula: DISTINCTCOUNT(Product ID)
- Business Importance: Measures product coverage.
